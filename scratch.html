Student Scramble

Our game is basically running around to avoid the homework papers. The objective is to last the longest as therewill be a timer at the top left. You, the student, will use WASD keys in order to move up, down, left and right. If hit by the homework, the game will be over as the student will have to redo. The longer you last, the harder the game will become.
